# Leon's Guide — Your Marketing Assistant

One page. Keep it open. Copy, paste, replace the [BRACKETS], send.

## Setup (once, with Yassine)

1. claude.ai → subscribe to Pro (~20€/month)
2. Open the Project called "[AGENCY NAME] Marketing" — always chat inside this Project
3. Your leads live in the Google Sheet: [SHEET LINK]

## Find new clients

```
Find 30 real estate agencies in [Athens] with phone, email and website. Table format.
```
```
Same but in [Thessaloniki], and search in Greek too.
```
```
Write a short email I can send to [AGENCY NAME FROM THE LIST] to propose my architecture services. Friendly, not salesy.
```

Then: copy the table into your Google Sheet, call/email them yourself, update the Status column.

## Watch competitors

```
Who are the main architecture agencies in Athens and what do they do to get clients?
```
```
Give me the Meta Ad Library link to see the ads of [COMPETITOR NAME], and tell me what to look for.
```
(Open the link, screenshot the interesting ads, paste them back to Claude for analysis.)
```
Write 10 ad variants for my agency based on what works for competitors. Different angles.
```

## Social media

```
Make my content calendar for [MONTH]: 15 ideas using my current projects: [describe 2-3 projects, e.g. "villa renovation in Glyfada, office fit-out in Kolonaki"].
```
```
Write the TikTok script and Instagram caption for idea number [3].
```
```
Last month, [what worked, e.g. "the before/after posts got the most views"]. Adjust next month's calendar.
```

## Rules of thumb

- Always chat **inside the Project** (it knows your business there).
- Attach photos of your projects when asking for content — better results.
- Claude prepares, **you** send/post/call. Nothing goes out automatically.
- Stuck? Just describe your problem in plain words. It's an assistant, not a form.
- Emergency / something broken: WhatsApp Yassine.
